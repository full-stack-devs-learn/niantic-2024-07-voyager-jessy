We are thrilled to have opened our 7th store in the Frederickson neighborhood of Pierce County! There are a lot of hungry residents due to a lack of dining options in the area and we’re excited to have filled that void in the community. Enjoy a glass of wine or a pint of beer around the fire table with your friends. Diners in the restaurant will enjoy watching their pizza being tossed into the air and baked in the Wood Stone oven behind the open kitchen line. And the kids will enjoy the games and candy crane in the arcade. Our Frederickson store also features a private party room for parties up to 25 people. Call now to reserve your next event!
